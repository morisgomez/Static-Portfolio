Centering Black Latinidad Report

This data brief anaylzes a myriad of parameters of the Afro-Latinx population experience in the United States. A few of many parameters studied include: trends in education, employment, and homeownership. The primary source of the report stems from microdata in the 2015-19 American Community Survey. I was assigned the literature review, data analysis and figure creation of uninsured rates and median household income. I was also in charge of compiling the final web-based report into an interactive and responsive one utilizing HTML, CSS and Datawrapper.
